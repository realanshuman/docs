TokenSupply lets you sell across multiple marketplaces from a single workspace. The platform separates two concepts: **Integrations** (connecting platforms) and **Sales Channel** (managing active listings). Understanding the difference helps you set up channels quickly and keep your listings organised.

## Integrations

**Integrations** (sidebar → **Integrations**) is where you connect external platforms — Shopify, G2A, and distributors. Each connector has its own detail page for credentials, connection status, and sync settings. You add, configure, and remove platform connections from this page.

## Sales Channel

**Sales Channel** (sidebar → **Sales Channel**) is where you manage active listings across all connected channels in one place. Filter by channel, toggle **Active** / **Auto Deliver** per listing, and review product mappings without jumping into each individual integration.

## Permissions

Both the **Integrations** and **Sales Channel** pages are restricted to **Super Admin** and **Admin** roles. Vendors and Support staff do not see either page in the sidebar.

<Note>
  New stores start with placeholder marketplace cards on the Integrations page. These guide you to connect your first channel before any listings appear.
</Note>

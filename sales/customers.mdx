TokenSupply's customer list keeps track of everyone you sell to — individuals and businesses alike. Each customer gets a sequential ID (`CUS-001`) and a profile with their full order and invoice history, so you always have context before or after a sale.

## Adding a customer

Navigate to **Sales → Customers → Add Customer**. Choose a customer type:

- **Individual** — a person; affects invoicing and tax treatment
- **Business** — a company; may have different invoicing requirements

TokenSupply assigns a sequential identifier automatically (`CUS-001`, `CUS-002`, and so on). You can search for customers by this code, their name, or their email address anywhere in the platform.

<Tip>
  You don't need to pre-create customers before placing an order. When creating a manual order or invoice, use the entity picker to create a customer inline — the new customer is saved to your list and selected automatically.
</Tip>

## Customer detail page

Opening a customer shows the standard detail layout with four tabs:

- **Overview** — customer type, contact details, and internal notes
- **Orders** — all transactions linked to this customer across all channels
- **Invoices** — all invoices issued to this customer
- **Activity** — a log of changes and actions on this customer record

You can add internal notes from the **Overview** tab. Notes are only visible to your team — they are never shown to the customer.

## Using customers in orders

When creating a manual order or invoice, search for a customer by name, email, or CUS-ID using the entity picker. If the customer doesn't exist yet, click **New Customer** in the picker to create one inline without navigating away from your current page.

## Individual vs Business

The customer type affects how invoices are generated and how tax is applied:

- **Individual** customers are typically treated as end consumers
- **Business** customers may have different tax rules, and their invoices can include company details

Choose the correct type when adding a customer to ensure invoices are generated correctly.

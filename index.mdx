## How TokenSupply works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your store">
    Sign up at [tokensupply.in](https://tokensupply.in/auth), verify your email, and set up your store name, currency, and business details in a few clicks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Catalog your products">
    Add digital products — game keys, gift cards, subscriptions — with SKUs, categories, and delivery settings. Your catalog becomes the central reference across all sales channels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Source from vendors">
    Add your suppliers, create purchase orders, and collect key stock directly through the platform. Vendors can upload stock files themselves via the vendor portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sell everywhere and deliver instantly">
    Connect Shopify or G2A, map your products, and enable auto-delivery. When an order is paid, TokenSupply reserves a key and sends the customer a branded delivery link — automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  TokenSupply uses a simple credit model: **1 key delivered = 1 credit**. The Free plan includes 50 credits. [See pricing](https://tokensupply.in/pricing) to find the right plan for your volume.
</Note>

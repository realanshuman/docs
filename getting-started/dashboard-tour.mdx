The dashboard is the first screen you see when you sign in. It gives you an immediate read on your store's health, one-click access to the most common actions, and a sidebar that gets you anywhere in the platform in a single click.

## Five KPI cards

Five cards summarise the current period in your store's default currency:

| Card | What it measures |
|---|---|
| **Revenue** | Net of refunds — completed orders only |
| **Orders** | Completed transactions across all channels |
| **Active Inventory** | Keys currently available for sale |
| **Open Tickets** | Issues awaiting action |
| **Credits Remaining** | Your billing usage at a glance |

All numeric values use tabular figures so columns stay aligned as numbers change.

## Quick actions

The dashboard toolbar gives you direct shortcuts to the four most common tasks without navigating the sidebar:

- **Create product** — start a new product listing
- **Add inventory** — load keys to an existing product
- **Log order** — create a manual order
- **Open ticket** — file a new support ticket

## Sidebar navigation

The sidebar is organised into three sections. Hover the sidebar edge to collapse it to icon-only mode.

| Section | What's inside |
|---|---|
| **Main** | Dashboard, Products, Purchases (Purchase Orders, Vendors), Sales (Customers, Invoices), Tickets, Messages, Transactions (All Transactions, Create Order), Analytics, Reports |
| **Sales Channels** | Integrations, Sales Channel |
| **Store** | Team, Settings, Templates, Billing & Plans |

<Info>
  The **Sales Channels** section — Integrations and Sales Channel — is only visible to **Super Admin** and **Admin** roles. Support staff and Vendors do not see these items.
</Info>

## Universal Search

Press `⌘K` on Mac or `Ctrl+K` on Windows to open the command palette. It expands to a 360px panel and lets you jump directly to any product, vendor, customer, purchase order, or transaction without leaving your current page.

<Tip>
  Universal Search is the fastest way to navigate when you know what you're looking for. Use it instead of clicking through the sidebar when you have a specific record in mind.
</Tip>

## Notifications

Click the **bell icon** in the topbar to open your in-app notification timeline. Notifications keep you informed about events across the platform:

| Type | What triggers it |
|---|---|
| **PO updates** | Status changes, invoice and stock approvals, due-date reminders |
| **Ticket events** | New tickets, replies, and resolutions |
| **Team** | Member joins (vendor or staff) and role changes |
| **Expiring inventory** | Keys expiring within your configured window |
| **Low stock** | Products falling under your configured threshold |
| **Stock file rejected** | A vendor needs to re-upload their stock file |
| **Low credits** | Billing approaching your plan limit |

Critical events always appear in-app, even if email delivery is paused.

**Configuring preferences:** go to **Settings → Notifications** to toggle email delivery per notification type and set your digest frequency — Instant, Hourly, Daily, or Weekly.

## Analytics

The **Analytics** page (sidebar → **Analytics**) is a full reporting dashboard built on charts. It sits next to **Reports**, which is a coming-soon placeholder for scheduled exports.

### Tabs

- **Overview** — revenue, orders, average order value, and channel mix over the selected range
- **Products** — top sellers and lowest-margin items, with drill-down per product
- **Vendors** — vendor performance, on-time delivery rate, and quality derived from ticket data
- **Customers** — Individual vs Business customer mix and repeat-purchase rate

### How revenue is calculated

Revenue on the Analytics page is **net of refunds** and covers **completed orders only**. Pending and failed orders are excluded from all calculations.

### Date range

Use the date picker to filter by Last 7 days, Last 30 days, Last 6 months, or a custom range. All KPI cards and charts update together.

<Note>
  Vendors who log in to the vendor portal see their own **Analytics** view with 6-month trend lines and KPIs scoped to their own activity.
</Note>

## Messages

The **Messages** module (sidebar → **Messages**) gives your team Slack-style channels and direct messages for internal coordination.

Key features:

- **Entity-linked threads** — attach an order, ticket, vendor, purchase order, or customer to a conversation. Linked entities open their detail page in one click.
- **Role badges** — color-coded badges (Admin / Support / Vendor) make it easy to see who you're talking to.
- **Vendor access** — vendors see a constrained version scoped only to threads they participate in.
- **Mobile** — collapses to a single-pane view for on-the-go access.

<Tip>
  Use entity-linked threads to keep discussion about a specific dispute or purchase order attached to the relevant record rather than scattered across channels.
</Tip>

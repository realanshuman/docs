---
title: "Set up your TokenSupply store and make your first sale"
description: "Create your store, configure business details, add your first product, load inventory keys, and complete your first digital goods sale."
sidebarTitle: "Quick Start"
---

You can go from zero to delivering your first digital key in under an hour. Follow these five steps to set up your store, load your catalog, and complete a sale.

<Note>
  The Free plan includes 50 credits. One credit is consumed each time a key is delivered to a customer. You won't be charged until you upgrade.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up and create your store">
    Go to [tokensupply.in](https://tokensupply.in) and create an account. After verifying your email, you are taken to the **Create Store** screen.

    Set your **store name**, **country**, and **default currency**. The account that creates the store becomes its **Super Admin** automatically — you'll have full access to every feature including billing and team management.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your business details">
    Open **Settings → General** and fill in your business information. This data appears on invoices, purchase order PDFs, and customer-facing emails.

    | Field | Purpose |
    |---|---|
    | **Store Name** | Your public brand name |
    | **Company Name** | Used on invoices and POs |
    | **Legal Name** | Formal entity name for documents |
    | **Support Email** | Shown to customers in delivery emails |
    | **Address** | Printed on invoices and POs |
    | **GST / PAN** | Tax identification for your business |
    | **Default Currency** | Applied to orders and analytics |
    | **Logo** | Appears in the topbar, customer portal, emails, and PDFs |

    Your **Order ID Format** (prefix + 5-digit sequence) is also configured on this tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your first product">
    Go to **Products → Add Product**. Work through the step-by-step form and fill in:

    - **Product Name** and **SKU**
    - **Brand**
    - **Category** — choose from the catalog (Game, Software, Gift Card, Subscription, and others)
    - **Product Type** — Key, Account, or Gift Card
    - **Delivery Time** — Instant, Within 1 hour, or Within 24 hours

    Save the product. It starts with no inventory, so your next step is to load keys.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Load inventory keys">
    Open the product you just created and click the **Inventory** tab. You have two options:

    - **Single key** — add one key inline with an optional expiry date and notes
    - **Bulk upload** — paste keys directly or upload a CSV file with one key per row

    TokenSupply checks for duplicate keys in real time and flags any conflicts before you save. Once your keys are loaded, the product is ready to sell.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect a sales channel or create a manual order">
    Choose how you want to make your first sale:

    **Connect a channel** — go to **Integrations** in the sidebar and connect Shopify, G2A, or another marketplace. Once connected, map your product on its **Sales Channels** tab and toggle **Auto Deliver** to fulfil orders automatically.

    **Create a manual order** — go to **Transactions → Create Order**, pick a customer (or create one inline), select the product and quantity, and confirm. A tokenised customer portal link is generated automatically and can be emailed to the customer at any time.

    <Tip>
      Manual orders are a good way to test the full delivery flow before connecting a live marketplace.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

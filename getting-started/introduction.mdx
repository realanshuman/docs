TokenSupply is the operating system for digital goods commerce. It gives you a single workspace to source from vendors, manage your key vault, sell across every marketplace, and resolve customer issues — without spreadsheets or stitched-together tools.

## Who it's for

TokenSupply is built for sellers of game keys, gift cards, software licenses, and subscriptions. Whether you're a solo operator running one storefront or a team managing multiple sales channels and a roster of vendors, TokenSupply fits the way digital goods businesses actually work.

## How the workflow fits together

Every digital goods sale follows the same arc. TokenSupply covers each stage:

| Stage | What you do in TokenSupply |
|---|---|
| **Catalog** | Add products with SKU, brand, category, and delivery type |
| **Source** | Create purchase orders with vendors and collect stock files |
| **Inventory** | Review, validate, and store keys in your encrypted key vault |
| **Sell** | List on Shopify, G2A, or other channels — or create manual orders |
| **Deliver** | Keys auto-deliver to customers via a branded portal link |
| **Support** | Manage tickets, issue replacements, and track resolutions |

## Team roles

Every person you add to your store gets a role that shapes what they see and can do.

| Role | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Super Admin** | Store owner — full access including billing and team management |
| **Admin** | Operations leads — team, billing, and integrations access |
| **Support** | Day-to-day staff — tickets, inventory, and orders |
| **Vendor** | External suppliers — limited to their own purchase orders and offers |

The account that creates the store is automatically assigned the **Super Admin** role.

Your TokenSupply store branding — logo and colour palette — appears consistently across every customer-facing surface: the admin app topbar, customer delivery portal, email headers, and generated PDFs.

## Uploading a logo

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open branding settings">
    Go to **Settings → General → Branding**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your logo">
    Click **Upload Logo** and select a PNG or SVG file. The file must be under 2 MB; 128×128 px is the recommended minimum size.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm placement">
    Your logo appears immediately in all surfaces listed below. Use **Change Logo** to swap it at any time, or **Remove** to revert to the default.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where your branding appears

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Admin topbar" icon="layout-dashboard">
    Your logo replaces the TokenSupply wordmark in the sidebar and top navigation bar of the admin workspace.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customer delivery portal" icon="monitor-smartphone">
    Customers see your logo when they open their tokenised delivery link to reveal their key.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transactional email headers" icon="mail">
    Order delivery emails, vendor invites, PO confirmations, and invoices all carry your logo in the header.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Invoice and PO PDFs" icon="file-text">
    Generated PDF documents — both customer invoices and vendor purchase orders — include your logo in the header.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
Use a PNG with a transparent background, or an SVG, for the cleanest results across both light and dark backgrounds. A square or horizontal logo at a minimum of 128×128 px avoids any upscaling blur.
</Tip>

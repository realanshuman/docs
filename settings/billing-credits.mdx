TokenSupply uses a simple, pay-as-you-deliver credit model: one key delivered consumes one credit. This page explains how credits work, what each plan includes, and how to monitor your usage.

## Credit model

Every time a key is delivered to a customer — whether through a connected marketplace like Shopify or G2A, or through a manual order — one credit is consumed.

**Refunds do not refund credits.** Once a key has been delivered, the credit is spent regardless of any subsequent refund or dispute.

## Plans

<Note>
The Free plan is free forever — no credit card required to get started.
</Note>

| Plan | Price | Credits | Highlights |
|------|-------|---------|------------|
| **Free** | $0 forever | 50 credits total | All features unlocked, single integration |
| **Pro** | $10/month (billed annually) | 200 credits/month | Multiple integrations, priority support, custom domain and branding, team chat |

## Billing page

Open **Billing & Plans** in the sidebar to manage your subscription and monitor usage. This page is visible to **Super Admins** and **Admins** only.

The overview shows:

- **Current plan** and price
- **Credits remaining** out of your plan allowance, with a visual progress bar
- **Wallet balance** — a pre-loaded balance you can use to buy credits or cover charges

## Upgrading to Pro

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing & Plans">
    Click **Billing & Plans** in the sidebar, or click the upgrade banner on the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the upgrade">
    Click **Upgrade** on the Pro plan card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Follow the checkout flow. Your plan changes take effect immediately after payment is confirmed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Monitoring usage

<Tip>
The **Credits Remaining** KPI card on the dashboard gives you an at-a-glance view of your usage without opening Billing & Plans. Check it regularly during high-volume periods to avoid running out mid-sale.
</Tip>

A full credit transaction history — showing every credit consumed, purchased, or adjusted with timestamps and running balances — is available under **Billing & Plans → History → Credit Transactions**.

Your account settings are separate from store settings — they control your personal profile, two-factor authentication, and session management. Access them via the user menu in the topbar or navigate to **/account**.

## Profile settings

Open **Account → Profile** to update your personal details:

- **First name** and **last name** — editable inline; click **Edit** next to any field
- **Language**, **timezone**, and **default currency** — apply to your personal workspace view
- **Email address** and **role** — shown read-only; email changes are handled through your authentication provider

## Enabling two-factor authentication (2FA)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open security settings">
    Go to **Account → Security**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start 2FA setup">
    Click **Set up two-factor authentication**. TokenSupply walks you through the TOTP setup process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan the QR code">
    Open your authenticator app and scan the QR code displayed on screen. The issuer appears as **tokensupply.in** in your app so it is easy to identify.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the verification code">
    Type the current 6-digit code from your authenticator app into the confirmation field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Verify & Enable**. After enabling, every sign-in requires a 6-digit TOTP code from your authenticator app in addition to your password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Use **1Password** or **Authy** for TOTP — both support encrypted backups and multi-device sync, so you are not locked out if you lose your phone. Google Authenticator also works but does not back up codes automatically.
</Tip>

## Password recovery

Use the **Reset Password** link on the sign-in page to trigger a recovery email. The recovery flow is MFA-aware and avoids lockouts for accounts with 2FA enabled.

You can also change your password directly from **Account → Security → Password** when you are signed in — enter your new password, confirm it, and click **Update Password**.

## Sessions

The **Active sessions** section under **Account → Security** shows your current session with the last sign-in timestamp. Click **Sign out** to terminate the session immediately, or **Sign out of all devices** to revoke all active sessions globally.

## Account deletion

<Warning>
Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All store data associated with the account is removed and cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

The **Delete Account** option is only available to Super Admins of a store-owning account. It is hidden for Vendor roles and team members on shared stores.

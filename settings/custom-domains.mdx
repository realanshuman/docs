By default your store is accessible at `tokensupply.in/s/{token_id}`. A custom domain lets you serve your store and customer delivery portal from your own URL — fully branded with automatic SSL.

## Adding a domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open domain settings">
    Go to **Settings → Domain** and click **Add Domain**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your domain">
    Type the domain you want to connect (e.g. `store.yourbrand.com`). Make sure you have access to the DNS settings at your domain registrar before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the required DNS records">
    Log in to your domain registrar and add the following records. Remove any existing `A` records for `@` and `www` first to avoid conflicts.

    | Type | Name | Value |
    |------|------|-------|
    | A | @ | 185.158.133.1 |
    | A | www | 185.158.133.1 |
    | TXT | _lovable | Shown on the Domain settings page after you enter your domain |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify and connect">
    Return to **Settings → Domain** and click **Verify & Connect**. TokenSupply checks your DNS records and provisions SSL automatically once verification passes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
SSL is provisioned automatically once DNS is verified — no manual certificate steps, CSR uploads, or renewal reminders are needed.
</Note>

## Domain statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Verifying** | Waiting for DNS propagation — check again in a few hours |
| **Active** | Live and serving your storefront and customer portal |
| **Failed** | DNS check failed; review your records at the registrar and click **Verify DNS** again |

<Info>
DNS propagation can take up to 72 hours depending on your registrar and TTL settings. The verification status updates automatically as propagation completes — you do not need to re-submit the form. Use a tool like [DNSChecker.org](https://dnschecker.org) to monitor progress across global nameservers.
</Info>

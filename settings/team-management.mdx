TokenSupply's team management lets you grow your operations by inviting staff with appropriate roles. Each role has a tailored experience — from full Super Admin access to the focused Support view.

## Roles and permissions

| Role | Team | Products | Analytics | Billing | Description |
|------|------|----------|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **Super Admin** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | Store owner — full access including billing and account deletion |
| **Admin** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | Operations leads — manage team, billing, and integrations |
| **Support** | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | Day-to-day operations: tickets, inventory, and order fulfilment |
| **Vendor** | ❌ | Own only | Own only | ❌ | External suppliers — limited to their own POs, offers, and products |

## Inviting team members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team">
    Click **Team** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the invite dialog">
    Click **Invite** in the top-right corner. This button is only visible to Super Admins and Admins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter email and select role">
    Type the invitee's email address and select either **Admin** or **Support** as their role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invite">
    Click **Send Invite**. The invitation appears in the **Invites** tab until the user signs up with that exact email address and joins the store.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Vendor invitations are sent from the vendor's detail page (**Purchases → Vendors → [Vendor] → Invite to Portal**), not from the Team → Invite flow. See [Managing Vendors](/vendors/managing-vendors) for details.
</Note>

## Team page tabs

| Tab | Visible to | What it shows |
|-----|-----------|---------------|
| **Members** | All roles | Staff members and their roles |
| **Vendors** | All roles | Vendor accounts that have portal access |
| **Invites** | Admin only | Pending and accepted invitations |
| **Activity** | Admin only | Administrative event audit log |

## Activity log

The Activity tab captures a full audit trail of administrative events including role changes, invites sent, and member removals. Each entry records the actor, target, timestamp, and any relevant details — useful for compliance and handover reviews.

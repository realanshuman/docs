TokenSupply's Settings module (sidebar → **Settings**) gives you five configuration tabs covering everything from your business identity and branding to email delivery and your public storefront.

## Settings tabs

| Tab | What lives here |
|-----|-----------------|
| **General** | Business details, branding (logo + colours), Order ID Format, store address and contact |
| **Email Setup** | SMTP host, credentials, from-name / from-email, test send |
| **Domain** | Custom domains and DNS verification |
| **Notifications** | Per-event email preferences and digest frequency |
| **Store Page** | Public storefront slug and visibility |

<Note>
**Templates** and **Billing & Plans** are separate top-level sidebar items — they are not Settings tabs.
</Note>

## Order ID format

Navigate to **Settings → General → Order ID Format** to customise how order IDs appear across your admin workspace, customer portal, and PDFs.

- **Prefix** — alphanumeric string of your choosing (e.g. `ORD`, `TS2026`)
- **Sequence** — fixed 5 digits, zero-padded (`00001` → `99999`)
- **Full format** — `{PREFIX}-{00001}`

Sequences are scoped per store and never reused, so every order ID across your store is permanently unique.

## Store page

Navigate to **Settings → Store Page** to control your public storefront.

- Edit your `token_id` slug — your storefront URL becomes `tokensupply.in/s/{token_id}` (or your custom domain once verified)
- Most fields auto-save on blur — no explicit save button needed
- Toggle the **Public / Private** visibility switch to hide or show your store page

<Info>
When your store page is set to private, the page is hidden from public browsing but tokenised customer portal links continue to work — your existing customers are never blocked.
</Info>

## Notifications

Navigate to **Settings → Notifications** to control how and when TokenSupply alerts you.

- Configure per-event email preferences for each notification type below
- Set digest frequency: **Instant**, **Hourly**, **Daily**, or **Weekly**

| Notification type | Triggered by |
|-------------------|-------------|
| PO updates | Status changes, invoice and stock approvals, due-date reminders |
| Ticket events | New tickets, replies, resolutions |
| Team | Member joins (vendor vs staff), role changes |
| Expiring inventory | Keys expiring within your configured window |
| Low stock | Products falling under your configured threshold |
| Stock file rejected | Vendor needs to re-upload a stock file |
| Low credits | Billing approaching your credit limit |
